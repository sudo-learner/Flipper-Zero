Step 1 — EM field onThe office door reader constantly emits a 125 kHz electromagnetic field — like an invisible bubble of energy. Any RFID card that enters this bubble gets powered by it wirelessly.
🪪 Real exampleYour college ID card, office key fob, DTC metro card — all use RFID. EM4100 fobs (very common in Indian apartments) are the easiest to clone.

Step 1 — Plug inA person plugs Flipper Zero into a laptop via USB-C. The laptop immediately starts USB enumeration — it asks the device "what are you?" The OS shows no warning.
⚠️Defense: Use a USB data blocker ("USB condom"). Always lock screen when away. Disable unused USB ports in BIOS.
Module 5

Bluetooth BLE

A coffee shop has many Bluetooth devices. Flipper scans them all silently. Watch how every device broadcasts itself — and how spam attacks exploit this.

1 · BLE broadcast
2 · Flipper scans
3 · Packet decode

Step 1 — BLE broadcastEvery BLE device — earbuds, fitness band, phone, speaker — constantly broadcasts small advertisement packets every 20–1000ms. The packet says: "I exist, my name is JBL Flip 6, I am a speaker."
🔒 DefenseTurn Bluetooth off when not using it. iOS 17.2+ and Android 14+ filter BLE spam. Use non-discoverable mode in settings.
